⚡ NEON DASHA high-octane, minimalist arcade survival game built with HTML5 Canvas.Neon Dash is a fast-paced "avoid-em-up" where you control a high-speed light pulse. Navigate through a digital void, dodge aggressive pink data-fragments, and harvest blue light bits to keep your signal alive.🕹️ Live DemoExperience the pulse directly in your browser. It features a physics-based "follow" mechanic that creates smooth, organic movement.Data BitsBlue glowing orbs. Collecting these increases your score and triggers a burst of particle effects.Frag-ObstaclesPink squares that spawn from the edges of the screen and track toward your last known position.Dynamic ScalingAs your score increases, the spawn rate and velocity of obstacles scale exponentially.🎮 ControlsThe game is designed to be fully responsive and cross-platform:Desktop (Keyboard): Use Arrow Keys or WASD for traditional arcade movement.Desktop (Mouse): Move your cursor; the pulse will interpolate toward the pointer position.Mobile (Touch): Drag your finger anywhere on the screen for precision navigation.🛠️ Technical Breakdown🎨 Visual EffectsMotion Trails: Achieved by drawing a semi-transparent background rectangle (rgba(10, 10, 18, 0.2)) every frame instead of clearing the canvas.Glow Engine: Uses Canvas shadowBlur and shadowColor properties to simulate a neon light aesthetic without heavy textures.Particle System: A custom class-based particle engine handles the explosions upon collision and collection.⚙️ PerformanceRequestAnimationFrame: Ensures smooth 60FPS synchronization with the monitor refresh rate.Linear Interpolation (Lerp): Used for player movement to provide a "weighty," premium feel rather than robotic positioning.📂 Project Structureneon-dash/
